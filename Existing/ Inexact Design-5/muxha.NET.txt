**Mux Based Half adder

.subckt muxha a b sum carry
.inc .\ntimux.NET
.inc .\ptimux.NET
.inc .\nti_gate.NET
.inc .\pti_gate.NET

Xpti_gate1 a apbar pti_gate
Xnti_gate1 a anbar nti_gate
Xptimux1 a anbar VDD2 f1 ptimux
Xntimux1 a VDD2 apbar f2 ntimux
Xntimux2 b a f2 f3 ntimux
Xptimux2 b f3 f1 sum ptimux

Xntimux3 a GND VDD2 f4 ntimux
Xptimux3 a GND VDD2 f5 ptimux
Xntimux4 b GND f5 f6 ntimux
Xptimux4 b f6 f4 carry ptimux


.ends
