**Mux based accurate Sum 

.subckt muxsum a b sum 
.inc .\ntimux.NET
.inc .\ptimux.NET
.inc .\nti_gate.NET
.inc .\pti_gate.NET
Xpti_gate1 a apbar pti_gate
Xnti_gate1 a anbar nti_gate
Xptimux1 a anbar VDD2 f1 ptimux
Xntimux1 a VDD2 apbar f2 ntimux
Xntimux2 b a f2 f3 ntimux
Xptimux2 b f3 f1 sum ptimux
.ends
